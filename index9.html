HTTP/3


HTTP/3 is the latest version of the Hypertext Transfer Protocol, designed for faster and more efficient communication between web servers and clients. It utilizes the QUIC transport protocol, enhancing performance by reducing latency and improving security. Notable features include multiplexing, stream prioritization, and improved error handling. HTTP/3 aims to enhance the overall web browsing experience by prioritizing speed and reliability in data exchange over the Internet.
